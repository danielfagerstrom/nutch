The P1 2010 Jump Bike is born and bred with the integrity to push the limits of physics at your favourite jumps.

 

 

Frame:
P.Series Reynolds 520 Cr-Mo, double-butted Headtube/Downtube, externally relieved Headtube, disc mount, 6mm offset horizontal drop out with integrated tensioner system, includes multispeed hardware, disc only
Fork:
Marzocchi DJ3, 80mm, Cr-Mo steerer, 32mm Cr-Mo stanchions, magnesium lower, fixed rate cartridge damping, coil/mcu with preload adj., "stiff" spring
Chainset:
Fuse tubular heat treated Cr-Mo 2 piece with hollow 19mm spindle
Chainrings:
CNC cut 6061, 25 tooth, Motostar design
Bottom Bracket:
Threaded Euro, alloy cups, 4x cartridge bearing, alloy spacers
Cassette:
Single speed, Cr-Mo, 1/8 inch x 12 tooth
Chain:
KMC Z510HX, 1/2 inch x 1/8 inch, anti-drop inner links
Pedals:
SBC Lo-A1, Alloy platform with removable pins, 9/16 inch
Front Brake:
Avid BB5, mechanical disc, 160mm G2 Clean Sweep rotor
Rear Brake:
Avid BB5, mechanical disc, 140mm G2 Clean Sweep rotor
Brake Levers:
Avid FR-5, alloy, two-finger, linear pull
Handlebars:
Alloy riser bar, 55mm rise, 680mm wide, 8 degree back sweep, 7 degree up
Stem:
SBC Aggro Stem, 3D forged/CNC, 4-bolt, 35mm length
Headset:
Integrated cartridge bearing, Campy type, 1-1/8 inch threadless
Grips:
Fuse BMX, single compound, closed end, 140mm
Rims:
P.Disc AM, double wall disc with eyelets, 36 hole
Front Hub:
Forged alloy, machined high/low flange and disc mount, 36 hole, nutted 3/8 inch axle
Rear Hub:
Forged alloy cassette, machined flanges and disc mount, double sealed, ground race, 36 hole, nutted 3/8 inch axle
Spokes:
2.0mm (14g) stainless
Front Tyre:
Specialized Rhythm Lite Control, 26x2.3 inch, 60TPI, aramid bead
Rear Tyre:
Specialized Rhythm Lite Control, 26x2.3 inch, 60TPI, aramid bead
Tubes:
Schrader valve
Saddle:
Specialized Fuse Lo Pro, 8mm rails, PVC bumpers front and rear
Seatpost:
Alloy two bolt, no offset, micro adjust, 25.4mm
Seat Binder:
Integrated with replaceable single bolt
Accessories:
Reflectors, owners manual, includes multispeed hardware
Weight:
Approx. 29.56lbs (13.41kg) (PLEASE NOTE: Weight is approximate and is provided by Evans Cycles as a guide only, actual weight may vary due to manufacturing processes and other factors)
